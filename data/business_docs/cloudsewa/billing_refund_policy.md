# CloudSewa Billing, Renewal, and Refund Policy

## 1. Billing Overview

CloudSewa services are billed based on selected plan, billing cycle, add-ons, licenses, and usage.

Supported billing cycles:

1. Monthly
2. Quarterly
3. Semi-annual
4. Annual
5. Multi-year for selected domains

## 2. Payment Methods

CloudSewa supports bank transfer, eSewa, Khalti, ConnectIPS, debit card, credit card, international card for selected customers, and manual invoice payment for corporate customers.

Services are activated only after payment confirmation unless customer has approved credit terms.

## 3. Invoice Generation

Invoices may be generated immediately after new order, before renewal date, after plan upgrade, after add-on purchase, after usage-based charge, or after manual service request.

Customers should pay invoices before due date to avoid service interruption.

## 4. Renewal Reminder

CloudSewa sends renewal reminders before service expiry.

Typical reminder schedule:

1. 14 days before due date
2. 7 days before due date
3. 3 days before due date
4. 1 day before due date
5. On due date

Failure to receive reminder does not remove customer responsibility to renew service.

## 5. Hosting Grace Period

For hosting and VPS services:

1. Service may remain active for up to 3 days after due date.
2. After grace period, service may be suspended.
3. After prolonged non-payment, service may be terminated.
4. Data recovery after termination is not guaranteed.

Grace period may not apply to customers with repeated late payment history.

## 6. Domain Renewal Billing

Domain renewal must be paid before expiry.

Domain renewal failure may cause website downtime, email downtime, DNS changes locked, redemption fee, and permanent domain loss.

Domain payments are generally non-refundable after successful registration or renewal because registry fees are committed.

## 7. Refund Eligibility

Refund may be available for duplicate payment, failed provisioning caused by CloudSewa, service not delivered after confirmed payment, billing error, wrong charge, customer cancels eligible hosting plan within trial window if offered, or SLA credit approved under SLA policy.

Refund is generally not available for domain registration, domain renewal, SSL certificate purchase after issuance, control panel license after activation, add-on license after activation, custom setup fee, migration fee after work has started, service suspended due to abuse, customer misconfiguration, or customer changed mind after using service.

## 8. Refund Processing Time

Refund timeline:

1. Wallet credit: within 24 hours after approval
2. Digital wallet: 1 to 3 business days
3. Bank transfer: 3 to 5 business days
4. Card reversal: 5 to 10 business days
5. International payment reversal: depends on payment processor

Support must not promise instant refund for bank or card payments.

## 9. Cancellation Request

Customers may request cancellation from dashboard or support ticket.

Cancellation options:

1. Immediate cancellation
2. Cancel at end of billing cycle
3. Cancel after migration
4. Cancel add-on only
5. Cancel renewal invoice

Immediate cancellation may remove access quickly. Customer should take backup before cancellation.

## 10. Chargeback Policy

If customer files chargeback or payment dispute, related service may be suspended, account may be reviewed, support may request proof, future payment methods may be restricted, and domain or license may be locked according to policy.

Customers should contact billing support before filing chargeback.
