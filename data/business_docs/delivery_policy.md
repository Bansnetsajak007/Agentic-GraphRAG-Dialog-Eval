# ChitoMart Delivery Policy

ChitoMart is a fictional Nepali quick-commerce company serving selected urban areas.

## Delivery Zones

Standard delivery is available inside Kathmandu Ring Road, Lalitpur core areas, Bhaktapur core areas, Kirtipur, and nearby supported neighborhoods shown in the app at checkout. Customers outside supported zones should be asked for their location before promising delivery.

## Delivery Time

Most grocery and household orders are delivered within 60 to 120 minutes after confirmation. Heavy rain, strikes, traffic blocks, festivals, or stock checks can delay delivery. If an order is delayed, support should check the order status and share a realistic update.

## Delivery Charges

Inside Kathmandu Ring Road, the standard delivery charge is NPR 60. Lalitpur, Bhaktapur, and Kirtipur orders usually have a delivery charge of NPR 80. Delivery charges may be higher for bulky orders or distant neighborhoods.

## Free Delivery

Orders of NPR 1,500 or more qualify for free delivery inside Kathmandu Ring Road. Outside Ring Road, free delivery may apply only when the checkout screen shows the offer.

## Failed Delivery

If the rider cannot reach the customer by phone after two attempts, the order may be marked failed. For prepaid orders, support should help reschedule or start refund handling according to the refund policy.
