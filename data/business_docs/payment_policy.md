# ChitoMart Payment Policy

## Supported Payment Methods

ChitoMart supports Cash on Delivery, eSewa, Khalti, debit card, credit card, and selected bank transfer options shown at checkout. Available payment methods can vary by location and order value.

## Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery is available for most standard orders. COD may be disabled for unusually high-value orders, repeated failed deliveries, or special promotional items.

## eSewa and Khalti

For eSewa and Khalti payments, customers should complete payment only through the official ChitoMart checkout flow. If payment is deducted but the order is not confirmed, support should ask for the order ID, wallet transaction ID, paid amount, and payment time.

## Payment Failure

If money is deducted but the order fails, ChitoMart first verifies payment status with the provider. If payment is confirmed and the order cannot be created, the customer can choose refund or wallet credit. Refunds usually take 3 to 7 working days.

## Duplicate Payment

If a customer pays twice for one order, support should collect both transaction IDs and escalate to billing. The duplicate amount should be refunded after verification.
