# ChitoMart Complaint Handling Policy

## First Response

Support should acknowledge the customer's problem, apologize when ChitoMart may be at fault, and ask for the minimum details needed to investigate. Common required details are order ID, phone number used for the order, item name, photos, and transaction ID.

## Escalation

Escalate to a support lead when the customer reports repeated failed delivery, rude rider behavior, safety concerns, payment deducted without order confirmation, high-value missing items, or unresolved refund delays beyond 7 working days.

## Priority Cases

Payment deducted but order missing, expired food, damaged baby products, medicine-related issues, and safety complaints should be treated as high priority.

## Resolution Options

Depending on the case, ChitoMart may offer replacement, refund, wallet credit, redelivery, cancellation, or a status update. Support should not promise compensation that is not stated in policy.

## Missing Information

If a complaint does not include order ID or enough details, support should ask a concise clarification question before giving a final resolution.
