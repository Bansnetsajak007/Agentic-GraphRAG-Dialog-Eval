# ChitoMart Product and Substitution Policy

## Product Availability

Product availability shown in the app is updated frequently but can change during picking. Fresh produce, bakery items, dairy, and imported goods may go out of stock quickly.

## Out of Stock Items

If an item is unavailable after order confirmation, ChitoMart may call or message the customer. The customer can choose a similar substitute, remove the item, or cancel the order if the unavailable item was essential.

## Substitutions

ChitoMart should not substitute an item without customer approval unless the customer has selected an automatic substitution preference in the app. Substitutes should be similar in type, size, and price where possible.

## Price Changes

The customer pays the price shown at checkout for confirmed items. If a substitute costs more, support should confirm with the customer before adding it.

## Product Quality

Fresh items should be reasonably fresh and usable. If fruits, vegetables, meat, or dairy arrive spoiled or unusable, the customer can report the issue under the refund policy.
