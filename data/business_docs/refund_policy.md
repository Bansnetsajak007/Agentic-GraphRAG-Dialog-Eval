# ChitoMart Refund Policy

## Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund when an item is missing, damaged, expired, significantly different from the listing, or unavailable after online payment has been completed. Refunds are not guaranteed for dislike of taste, wrong customer selection, or change of mind after successful delivery.

## Damaged or Expired Items

For damaged, leaked, broken, or expired items, customers should contact ChitoMart support within 24 hours of delivery. Support should ask for the order ID and a clear photo when possible. Eligible cases may receive refund, replacement, or wallet credit.

## Wrong Item Policy

If ChitoMart sends the wrong item, support should apologize, collect the order ID and item details, and arrange replacement or refund. Customers should keep the wrong item unused until the rider or support team confirms the next step.

## Missing Items

For missing items, support should verify the packing record. If the item was billed but not delivered, ChitoMart should refund the item value or deliver the missing item if available.

## Refund Timeline

Wallet credits are usually completed within 24 hours after approval. eSewa, Khalti, card, and bank refunds usually take 3 to 7 working days depending on the payment provider.
