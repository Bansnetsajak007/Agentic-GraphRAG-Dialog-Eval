# ChitoMart Company Profile

## 1. Company Overview

ChitoMart is a fictional quick-commerce and e-commerce platform that delivers groceries, daily essentials, packaged food, household items, personal care products, baby care products, basic electronics accessories, and selected fresh items across Kathmandu Valley.

ChitoMart focuses on fast delivery, transparent pricing, reliable customer support, and simple return/refund handling. The platform serves customers through its mobile app, website, WhatsApp support, and call-center support.

## 2. Supported Product Categories

ChitoMart currently supports the following product categories:

1. Grocery and pantry items
2. Fresh fruits and vegetables
3. Dairy and bakery items
4. Beverages and packaged food
5. Household cleaning items
6. Personal care and hygiene products
7. Baby care products
8. Pet care essentials
9. Stationery and office essentials
10. Basic electronics accessories such as chargers, cables, earphones, batteries, and power banks

## 3. Unsupported Product Categories

ChitoMart does not currently sell or deliver:

1. Prescription medicines
2. Alcoholic products
3. Tobacco or nicotine products
4. Hazardous chemicals
5. Fireworks or explosive items
6. Live animals
7. High-value electronics such as mobile phones, laptops, televisions, or refrigerators
8. Items requiring professional installation
9. Products restricted by local law or platform safety policy

## 5. Support Hours

Standard support hours:

- Sunday to Friday: 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Public holidays: Limited support from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Urgent delivery, payment, and damaged-item issues are prioritized during all support hours.

## 6. Standard Support Tone

ChitoMart support replies should be:

1. Polite
2. Clear
3. Short but complete
4. Helpful
5. Grounded in company policy
6. Friendly in Romanized Nepali or English depending on the customer's language

Support agents should not overpromise. If the policy does not allow something, the agent must explain the reason clearly and offer the nearest valid option.

## 7. Important Customer Support Principle

When answering customers, always identify:

1. What issue the customer is reporting
2. Whether order ID is needed
3. Whether product photo or proof is needed
4. Whether the policy allows refund, replacement, return, or support escalation
5. Whether the customer needs a direct answer or a clarification question

If necessary information is missing, ask a clarification question instead of guessing.
