# ChitoMart Delivery Policy

## 1. Delivery Coverage

ChitoMart currently delivers inside Kathmandu Valley. Delivery is available in selected areas of Kathmandu, Lalitpur, and Bhaktapur.

Primary delivery zones:

1. Kathmandu Central Zone
2. Kathmandu Outer Zone
3. Lalitpur Zone
4. Bhaktapur Zone
5. Priority Express Zone

Delivery availability may depend on traffic, weather, product availability, rider availability, local restrictions, and order volume.

## 7. Delivery Time Rules

The estimated delivery time starts after the order is confirmed.

Order confirmation means:

1. The customer has placed the order successfully.
2. Payment is verified or Cash on Delivery is selected.
3. Ordered items are available.
4. The nearest fulfillment hub accepts the order.

Estimated delivery time may increase during heavy rain, road blockage, traffic congestion, local strikes or restrictions, festival peak periods, high order volume, rider shortage, and product replacement confirmation delay.

## 8. Same-Day Delivery

Most ChitoMart orders are delivered on the same day if placed before 7:30 PM.

Orders placed after 7:30 PM may be delivered the same evening if rider availability exists, otherwise delivery may be moved to the next day.

Fresh items ordered after 7:00 PM may be unavailable for same-day delivery.

## 9. Delivery Failure

A delivery may fail if customer phone is unreachable, customer address is incomplete, customer is unavailable at delivery location, delivery location is outside service area, road access is blocked, customer refuses delivery without a valid reason, or a Cash on Delivery customer refuses to pay.

If delivery fails due to customer unavailability, ChitoMart may attempt one redelivery. A redelivery charge may apply.

## 10. Redelivery Rules

Redelivery is allowed if the original delivery failed due to customer missed rider call, address needed correction, customer requested a later delivery time, or rider could not access the building or location.

Redelivery charge:

- Same zone redelivery: NPR 50
- Different zone redelivery: normal delivery charge applies again

Perishable items such as dairy, bakery, fruits, and vegetables may not be eligible for redelivery if product quality cannot be guaranteed.

## 11. Customer Address Rules

Customers must provide full name, active phone number, city or municipality, area name, street or landmark, house number or nearby reference, and delivery instructions if needed.

If the address is incomplete, support should ask the customer for missing information instead of confirming delivery time.

## 12. Delivery Charge Disputes

If a customer says delivery charge is incorrect, support should check customer area, order subtotal, delivery type, promotion or coupon applied, free delivery threshold, and whether heavy item surcharge applies.

If the delivery charge is incorrectly applied due to system error, ChitoMart may adjust the charge or issue wallet credit.

## 13. Heavy Item Surcharge

Heavy item surcharge may apply to rice sacks above 10 kg, large detergent packs, bulk beverage cartons, multiple cooking oil jars, and large household supplies.

Heavy item surcharge: NPR 50 to NPR 150 depending on order weight and delivery distance.

Heavy item surcharge is shown before checkout whenever possible.
