# ChitoMart Complaint Handling SOP

## 1. Complaint Handling Overview

ChitoMart receives complaints related to late delivery, wrong item, damaged item, missing item, payment issue, rude rider, bad product quality, coupon issue, cancellation issue, and refund delay.

Support agents must respond calmly, verify the issue, check policy, and provide the correct resolution path.

## 2. Complaint Priority Levels

### Priority P1: Critical

Examples:

1. Customer reports unsafe product
2. Customer reports serious health risk
3. Customer reports payment deducted multiple times
4. Customer reports rider misconduct or harassment
5. Customer threatens legal complaint
6. High-value order issue above NPR 10,000

Response target: within 15 minutes during support hours
Escalation required: Yes

### Priority P2: High

Examples:

1. Missing item
2. Damaged item
3. Wrong item
4. Expired product
5. Order delayed more than 2 hours
6. Refund not received after promised timeline

Response target: within 30 minutes during support hours
Escalation required: If unresolved in first response

### Priority P3: Normal

Examples:

1. Product availability question
2. Delivery fee question
3. Coupon question
4. Invoice request
5. Change address before dispatch
6. Cancellation before packing

Response target: within 2 business hours

### Priority P4: Low

Examples:

1. General inquiry
2. Feedback
3. Feature request
4. Product suggestion
5. Non-urgent account question

Response target: within 24 hours

## 3. Complaint Verification Steps

Support agent should verify customer identity, order ID, order date, product involved, delivery status, payment method, photos or screenshots, policy eligibility, previous support history, and whether escalation is needed.

## 4. Late Delivery Complaint

When customer reports late delivery, support should check order ID, order status, rider assignment, rider location if available, estimated delivery time, delay reason, and whether order includes perishable items.

Possible resolutions:

1. Provide updated ETA
2. Apologize and continue delivery
3. Offer cancellation if delay is excessive and eligible
4. Offer wallet credit for major inconvenience
5. Escalate to operations team

Do not promise exact rider arrival time unless confirmed by system.

## 5. Wrong Item Complaint

When customer reports wrong item, ask for order ID, photo of received item, photo of invoice or package label, and description of expected item.

Possible resolutions: correct item delivery, replacement, refund, or wallet credit.

## 6. Damaged Item Complaint

When customer reports damaged item, ask for order ID, product photo, packaging photo, delivery time, and whether item was damaged at delivery or noticed later.

Possible resolutions: replacement, refund, partial refund, or wallet credit.

## 7. Missing Item Complaint

When customer reports missing item, ask for order ID, missing item name, invoice photo if available, and package photo if available.

Check packing record, rider handover record, invoice, and product stock movement.

Possible resolutions: deliver missing item, refund missing item value, or wallet credit.

## 8. Rude Rider or Misconduct Complaint

Rider misconduct must be handled seriously.

Ask for order ID, rider name if visible, delivery time, description of incident, and call recording or screenshot if available.

Escalate to operations team.

Do not blame the customer. Do not dismiss the complaint.

Possible resolutions: apology, internal investigation, rider warning or action, wallet credit in service recovery cases, and manager callback.

## 9. Refund Delay Complaint

When customer says refund has not arrived, check refund approval date, refund method, transaction reference, expected refund timeline, and whether bank/payment partner delay exists.

If timeline has not passed, explain expected timeline.

If timeline has passed, escalate to payment team.

## 10. Complaint Closure Rules

A complaint can be closed only when customer receives clear answer, refund/replacement/cancellation is processed, escalation ticket is created, customer is informed about next steps, and internal notes are updated.

Do not close unresolved complaints without customer update.

## 11. Escalation Notes Format

Support agent should write escalation note like:

```text
Order ID:
Customer issue:
Product involved:
Evidence received:
Policy checked:
Customer requested resolution:
Recommended action:
Priority:
```

## 12. Standard Complaint Responses

Customer query:
"tapaiko service dherai slow cha"

Correct response:
"Sorry for the inconvenience. Tapai ko experience ramro vayena bhanera dukha lagyo. Kripaya order ID share garnu hola, ma delay reason check garera update dinchu."

Customer query:
"rider rude thiyo"

Correct response:
"Sorry, yo serious concern ho. Kripaya order ID, delivery time, ra incident ko short detail share garnu hola. Hami operations team ma escalate garera review garchau."

Customer query:
"refund aayena ajhai"

Correct response:
"Refund delay ko lagi sorry. Kripaya order ID or refund reference share garnu hola. Ma refund status check garera payment team ma escalate garna parcha ki confirm garchu."
