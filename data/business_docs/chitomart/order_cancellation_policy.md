# ChitoMart Order Cancellation Policy

## 1. Cancellation Overview

Customers may cancel orders depending on order status, product type, payment status, and rider assignment status.

ChitoMart supports cancellation when it is operationally possible and when cancellation does not create product loss or delivery abuse.

## 2. Order Status Definitions

### 2.1 Pending Payment

Order is created but payment is not completed.

Cancellation allowed: Yes
Cancellation fee: No
Refund required: No, unless payment was deducted later

### 2.2 Payment Received

Payment is completed but order is not yet packed.

Cancellation allowed: Yes
Cancellation fee: No for normal items
Refund method: Original payment method or wallet credit

### 2.3 Order Confirmed

Order is accepted by fulfillment hub.

Cancellation allowed: Usually yes if packing has not started
Cancellation fee: Usually no

### 2.4 Packing Started

Items are being picked and packed.

Cancellation allowed: Case-by-case
Cancellation fee: May apply for fresh or special items

### 2.5 Ready for Dispatch

Order is packed and ready for rider pickup.

Cancellation allowed: Limited
Cancellation fee: May apply

### 2.6 Out for Delivery

Rider has picked up the order.

Cancellation allowed: Usually no
Exception: Major delay, wrong address detected early, rider unable to deliver, or support-approved case

### 2.7 Delivered

Cancellation allowed: No
Customer must use return/refund policy if there is an issue.

## 3. Cancellation Before Dispatch

Customers may cancel without charge before dispatch if order was placed by mistake, customer selected wrong address, customer selected wrong payment method, customer wants to edit item list, customer no longer needs the order, or delivery estimate is longer than expected and order is not packed.

## 4. Cancellation After Dispatch

Cancellation after dispatch is usually not allowed because rider and fulfillment costs are already incurred.

Exception cases:

1. Delivery is delayed beyond 2 hours from maximum estimated time.
2. Rider cannot locate customer due to system address error.
3. Product becomes unavailable after dispatch.
4. Order contains wrong items identified before delivery.
5. Support approves cancellation due to valid reason.

Cancellation after dispatch may lead to non-refundable delivery charge.

## 5. Cancellation of Fresh Items

Fresh items such as fruits, vegetables, dairy, bakery, and chilled items may not be cancellable once packing starts.

Reason: fresh item quality and resale safety cannot always be guaranteed after packing.

If fresh item cancellation is accepted, refund may be partial depending on product condition and order status.

## 6. Cancellation Due to Product Unavailability

If an item becomes unavailable after order placement, ChitoMart may remove unavailable item and refund item value, suggest substitute product, delay order until item is restocked, or cancel full order if customer does not want partial delivery.

Customer confirmation is required before substitution unless the customer selected "allow substitutions" during checkout.

## 7. Cancellation Due to Late Delivery

If delivery is delayed beyond promised window, customer may request cancellation.

Support should check order status, rider location, delay reason, product type, whether the order is perishable, and whether customer still wants the order.

If delay is caused by ChitoMart and order is still returnable, cancellation may be approved.

## 8. Refund After Cancellation

Refund timeline depends on payment method:

- Wallet refund: within 24 hours
- Digital wallet refund: 1 to 3 business days
- Bank refund: 3 to 5 business days
- Card reversal: 5 to 10 business days
- COD order: no refund unless customer prepaid partially

Delivery charge may not be refunded if the order was already dispatched.

## 9. Repeated Cancellation Abuse

ChitoMart may restrict cancellation privileges if a customer repeatedly cancels after dispatch or refuses COD orders without valid reason.

Possible actions:

1. Require prepaid payment for future orders
2. Disable COD
3. Apply cancellation fee
4. Temporarily restrict account
5. Review account by risk team

## 10. Standard Cancellation Support Responses

Customer query:
"order cancel garna milcha?"

Correct response:
"Order status herera cancel garna milcha ki mildaina confirm huncha. Kripaya order ID share garnu hola, ma status check garera cancellation option confirm garchu."

Customer query:
"rider le pickup garisakyo, cancel garna milcha?"

Correct response:
"Rider pickup bhayepachi cancellation normally allowed hudaina. Tara major delay or valid issue cha bhane support team le case review garna sakcha."

Customer query:
"payment gareko order cancel gare, refund kahile aaucha?"

Correct response:
"Cancellation approve bhayepachi refund method anusar timeline farak huncha. Wallet ma 24 hours vitra, digital wallet ma 1-3 business days, bank ma 3-5 business days, card ma 5-10 business days lagna sakcha."
