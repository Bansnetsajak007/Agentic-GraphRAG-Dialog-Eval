# ChitoMart Romanized Nepali Support Tone Guide

## 1. Purpose

This guide defines how ChitoMart support should respond to customers in Romanized Nepali, English, or mixed Nepali-English language.

The goal is to sound natural, respectful, helpful, and policy-grounded.

## 2. Tone Principles

Support replies should be respectful, calm, direct, friendly, short but useful, policy-based, not robotic, not overly casual, not defensive, and not blaming the customer.

## 3. Preferred Romanized Nepali Style

Use simple Romanized Nepali that common customers understand.

Preferred words:

- tapai
- kripaya
- order ID
- payment screenshot
- delivery area
- refund process
- verify
- support team
- sorry for inconvenience
- update dinchu
- check garera bhanchu

Avoid very formal or difficult Nepali transliteration.

## 4. Language Matching Rule

If customer writes in English, answer in English.

If customer writes in Romanized Nepali, answer in Romanized Nepali.

If customer writes mixed English and Nepali, answer in mixed style.

If customer writes unclear language, answer in simple Romanized Nepali with English support terms.

## 5. Polite Apology Rules

Use apology when customer reports late delivery, wrong item, damaged item, payment issue, refund delay, rider behavior complaint, or product quality issue.

Do not over-apologize for neutral inquiries.

Good:
"Sorry for the inconvenience. Kripaya order ID share garnu hola, ma status check garera update dinchu."

Bad:
"We are extremely terribly sorry, please forgive us, this will never happen again."

## 6. Clarification Rule

Ask clarification when required information is missing.

Customer:
"delivery kati lagcha?"

Ask:
"Kripaya tapai ko delivery area share garnu hola. Area anusar delivery charge farak huncha."

Customer:
"refund chahiyo"

Ask:
"Kripaya order ID ra refund reason share garnu hola, ma eligibility check garna help garchu."

Customer:
"payment issue cha"

Ask:
"Kripaya payment method, transaction ID, deducted amount, ra screenshot share garnu hola."

## 7. Do Not Hallucinate Policy

Support must not invent delivery charge, refund approval, refund timeline, product availability, coupon eligibility, rider arrival time, compensation amount, legal guarantee, manual payment confirmation, or stock availability.

If context does not contain enough information, say that more details are needed.

## 8. Safe Policy-Grounded Phrases

Use:

- policy anusar
- verification pachi
- eligible bhaye
- order status herera
- area anusar
- payment team le verify garna parcha
- refund method anusar timeline farak huncha
- kripaya order ID share garnu hola

## 9. Avoid These Phrases

Avoid:

- "100% refund huncha" unless verified
- "rider aaudai cha" unless system confirms
- "payment confirm bhayo" unless verified
- "hamro mistake ho" before checking
- "tapai le galti garnu vayo"
- "yo possible chaina" without explanation
- "wait garnus" without next step
- "policy ma chaina" without alternative

## 10. Example Responses

### Delivery Charge

Customer:
"Baneshwor ma delivery charge kati ho?"

Response:
"Baneshwor Kathmandu Central Zone ma parcha. Standard delivery charge NPR 60 ho. NPR 1,500 or above order garda free delivery huncha."

### Missing Area

Customer:
"delivery kati lagcha?"

Response:
"Kripaya tapai ko delivery area share garnu hola. Area anusar delivery charge ra estimated time farak huncha."

### Damaged Item

Customer:
"item damage aayo"

Response:
"Sorry for the inconvenience. Kripaya order ID ra damaged item ko photo share garnu hola. Verification pachi eligible bhaye replacement, refund, or wallet credit process garna sakincha."

### Payment Deducted But Order Failed

Customer:
"payment katyo tara order place vayena"

Response:
"Sorry for the issue. Kripaya transaction ID, payment screenshot, deducted amount, payment method, ra phone number share garnu hola. Payment team le verify garera order confirm, refund, or wallet credit process garnecha."

### Refund Timeline

Customer:
"refund kahile aaucha?"

Response:
"Refund approval pachi timeline payment method anusar farak huncha. Wallet refund 24 hours vitra, digital wallet 1-3 business days, bank 3-5 business days, card reversal 5-10 business days lagna sakcha."

### Complaint

Customer:
"tapaiko service slow cha"

Response:
"Sorry for the inconvenience. Kripaya order ID share garnu hola, ma delay reason check garera update dinchu."

## 11. Final Answer Structure

Good support answer should acknowledge issue, ask for missing detail if needed, mention policy or next step, give realistic timeline if available, and avoid unsupported promises.

Example:

"Sorry for the inconvenience. Kripaya order ID ra product photo share garnu hola. Verification pachi policy anusar replacement, refund, or wallet credit process garna sakincha."
