# ChitoMart Refund and Return Policy

## 1. Policy Overview

ChitoMart allows returns, replacements, refunds, and wallet credits depending on product type, issue type, product condition, and reporting time.

The goal of this policy is to protect customers from damaged, expired, missing, incorrect, or misrepresented items while also preventing misuse of the return system.

## 2. Available Resolution Types

Depending on the case, ChitoMart may offer full refund, partial refund, product replacement, missing item delivery, wallet credit, coupon compensation, support escalation, or no refund if the case is not eligible.

## 3. Refund Methods

Refunds may be processed through original payment method, ChitoMart wallet credit, bank transfer, digital wallet transfer, or cash refund in rare approved cases.

Original payment method is preferred when technically possible.

## 4. Refund Processing Time

Standard refund processing time:

- ChitoMart wallet credit: within 24 hours after approval
- Digital wallet refund: 1 to 3 business days after approval
- Bank transfer: 3 to 5 business days after approval
- Card payment reversal: 5 to 10 business days depending on bank/payment partner
- Cash refund: handled case-by-case

Support must not promise instant bank or card refunds.

## 5. Return Window by Product Type

### 5.1 Fresh Fruits and Vegetables

Return or refund request must be raised within 6 hours of delivery.

Eligible issues: rotten item, spoiled item, completely wrong item, missing item, item damaged during delivery, or quantity significantly less than ordered.

Not eligible: taste preference, minor size variation, customer changed mind, customer stored item improperly after delivery, or request made after 6 hours.

### 5.2 Dairy and Bakery Items

Return or refund request must be raised within 6 hours of delivery.

Eligible issues: expired product, broken packaging, spoiled product, wrong product, or missing item.

Not eligible: customer opened and consumed product, taste preference, improper storage after delivery, or request made after 6 hours.

### 5.3 Packaged Grocery Items

Return or refund request must be raised within 7 days of delivery.

Eligible issues: expired item, damaged package, wrong item, missing item, product does not match description, or sealed product received in unusable condition.

Not eligible: opened or used product without defect, customer changed mind after opening, product damaged after delivery, or request made after 7 days.

### 5.4 Personal Care and Hygiene Products

Return or refund request must be raised within 7 days of delivery.

Eligible only if item is sealed and unused, wrong item was delivered, product is expired, packaging was damaged before delivery, or product does not match description.

Not eligible if seal is broken, product is used, product is opened for trial, or customer changed mind after opening.

### 5.5 Baby Care Products

Return or refund request must be raised within 7 days of delivery.

Eligible if wrong item was delivered, product is expired, packaging is damaged, product does not match description, or item is sealed and unused.

Not eligible if hygiene seal is broken, product is used, or customer ordered wrong size and opened the package.

### 5.6 Electronics Accessories

Return or refund request must be raised within 7 days of delivery.

Eligible issues: product is dead on arrival, product does not work, wrong product delivered, missing accessory, or product does not match description.

Verification may be required before refund approval.

Not eligible: physical damage after delivery, water damage, burn damage, misuse, no original packaging, or request after 7 days.

## 6. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are generally non-returnable unless wrong, expired, damaged, or defective at delivery: fresh produce, dairy and bakery after allowed window, personal hygiene items after seal is broken, baby hygiene items after seal is broken, opened packaged food, custom or special order items, clearance sale items marked final sale, free gift items, and products damaged due to customer misuse.

## 7. Required Proof for Refund or Replacement

Depending on the issue, support may ask for order ID, product photo, package photo, expiry date photo, delivery invoice photo, short video showing defect, payment screenshot, and customer phone number.

For missing item claims, ChitoMart may check packing logs, rider handover records, and order photos where available.

## 8. Damaged Item Policy

If a product arrives damaged, customer must report it within the allowed return window.

Support should ask for order ID, photo of damaged item, photo of outer packaging, and description of issue.

If damage is verified, ChitoMart may offer replacement, full refund, wallet credit, or partial refund if customer agrees to keep usable item.

## 9. Wrong Item Policy

If the customer receives the wrong item, ChitoMart will verify the order and delivered product.

Customer must report wrong item within 6 hours for fresh, dairy, and bakery items, and within 7 days for packaged and non-perishable items.

If verified, ChitoMart may offer correct item delivery, replacement, full refund, or wallet credit.

If the wrong item is collected back, the product must be unused and in returnable condition unless it was delivered damaged.

## 10. Missing Item Policy

If an item is missing from the order, customer should report it within 24 hours of delivery.

Support should check order details, invoice, packing record, rider handover status, and customer proof if available.

If missing item is verified, ChitoMart may deliver missing item, refund missing item value, or add wallet credit.

Delivery of missing item depends on rider availability and item availability.

## 11. Customer Changed Mind

Customer changed mind cases are handled differently from product defect cases.

For sealed, unused, non-perishable products, return may be accepted within 7 days.

For fresh, dairy, bakery, opened food, hygiene, baby care, and final-sale items, change-of-mind return is not normally accepted.

If change-of-mind return is approved, delivery fee is not refunded and return pickup charge may apply.

## 12. Refund Rejection Cases

Refund may be rejected if customer reports after allowed time window, product was used or consumed, product was damaged after delivery, customer cannot provide required proof, product is non-returnable, customer changed mind for perishable item, issue cannot be verified, customer violated platform policy, order was already refunded, or claim appears fraudulent or repeated suspiciously.

## 13. Refund Escalation

Escalate the case to senior support if customer claims high-value missing item, payment was deducted but order failed, customer reports repeated delivery issues, customer reports health/safety concern, customer threatens legal action, customer reports rider misconduct, refund value is above NPR 5,000, or customer has multiple refund claims in short period.
