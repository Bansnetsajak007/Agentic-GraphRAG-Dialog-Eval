# ChitoMart Account and Customer Policy

## 1. Account Creation

Customers may create a ChitoMart account using phone number, email address, or social login where available.

Phone number verification may be required before placing an order.

## 2. Customer Profile Information

Customer profile may include name, phone number, email address, delivery addresses, order history, wallet balance, saved payment methods, support history, coupon eligibility, and preference settings.

## 3. Multiple Accounts

Customers should not create multiple accounts to misuse first-order coupons, referral rewards, or promotional offers.

If misuse is detected, ChitoMart may disable coupons, remove promotional wallet credit, disable COD, temporarily restrict account, or cancel suspicious orders.

## 4. Address Management

Customers may save multiple delivery addresses.

Each address should include area, street, landmark, house number if available, contact person, phone number, and delivery instruction.

Incomplete address may delay delivery.

## 5. Phone Number Change

If customer wants to change registered phone number, support may ask for current phone number, new phone number, recent order ID, and identity verification where required.

Phone number change may affect coupon eligibility and order history access.

## 6. Account Restriction

Account may be restricted due to repeated fake orders, repeated COD refusal, coupon abuse, payment dispute abuse, fraudulent refund claims, abusive behavior toward staff or riders, or violation of platform terms.

## 7. COD Restriction

Cash on Delivery may be disabled if customer repeatedly refuses orders, is unreachable multiple times, cancels after rider dispatch repeatedly, has suspicious order pattern, or has high unpaid dues.

Customer may still be allowed to order using prepaid payment.

## 8. Customer Data Correction

Customer may request correction of name, phone number, email, or delivery address.

Support should verify the account before modifying sensitive information.
