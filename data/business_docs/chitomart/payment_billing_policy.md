# ChitoMart Payment and Billing Policy

## 1. Supported Payment Methods

ChitoMart supports Cash on Delivery, ChitoMart Wallet, eSewa, Khalti, ConnectIPS, debit card, credit card, bank transfer for selected corporate orders, promo wallet balance, and gift voucher or coupon credit.

Available payment methods may vary depending on order value, customer account status, risk checks, and service area.

## 2. Cash on Delivery Rules

Cash on Delivery is available for most standard orders.

COD may not be available for high-value orders above NPR 10,000, customers with repeated delivery refusal, customers with repeated cancellation after dispatch, selected electronic accessories, corporate bulk orders, areas with temporary COD restrictions, and special festival pre-order campaigns.

ChitoMart may require prepaid payment for accounts with repeated failed delivery history.

## 3. Digital Wallet Payment Rules

When customer pays using eSewa, Khalti, or another digital wallet, the order is confirmed only after payment success is received by ChitoMart.

If payment is pending, order may remain on hold.

If payment fails, customer may retry payment or choose another method.

## 4. Card Payment Rules

Card payment confirmation depends on banking network and payment gateway response.

If amount is deducted but ChitoMart does not receive confirmation, the order may remain failed or pending until payment reconciliation.

Support must not manually mark card order as paid unless verified by payment team.

## 5. Payment Deducted but Order Failed

If customer reports money deducted but order failed, support must collect customer phone number, order ID if generated, payment method, transaction ID, payment screenshot, deducted amount, and date and time of transaction.

Resolution options:

1. Confirm order if payment is verified and items are available
2. Refund payment if order cannot be confirmed
3. Add wallet credit if customer agrees
4. Escalate to payment team

Standard resolution time: 1 to 3 business days
Complex payment gateway cases: up to 5 business days

## 6. Duplicate Payment

Duplicate payment may occur if customer pays twice for the same order.

If duplicate payment is verified, ChitoMart will refund extra payment through original payment method or wallet credit.

Required information: order ID, both transaction IDs, payment screenshots, payment method, and customer phone number.

## 7. Partial Payment

Partial payment may occur when wallet balance covers part of order, coupon covers part of order, customer uses wallet plus COD, or corporate account uses credit limit.

Refund for partial payment follows the source of payment.

Example: If NPR 500 was paid by wallet and NPR 1,000 by card, refund may be split between wallet and card.

## 8. Invoice and Billing

Every successful order generates an invoice.

Invoice includes order ID, customer name, delivery address, ordered items, quantity, item price, discounts, delivery charge, payment method, total payable amount, and tax information where applicable.

Customer may request invoice copy through support.

## 9. Coupon and Discount Rules

Coupons may have minimum order value, expiry date, product category restriction, area restriction, payment method restriction, first-order-only restriction, one-time-use restriction, and maximum discount cap.

If a coupon is not working, support should check coupon code spelling, expiry date, minimum order value, customer eligibility, product category, payment method, and whether coupon was already used.

## 10. Refund of Coupon Discount

Coupons are not usually refunded as cash.

If order is cancelled due to ChitoMart issue, coupon may be reissued or replaced.

If customer cancels voluntarily, coupon may not be restored.

## 11. Wallet Credit

Wallet credit may be used for refund compensation, missing item refund, service recovery, promotional credit, and referral reward.

Wallet credit cannot always be withdrawn as cash. Withdrawal depends on credit type.

Refund wallet credit may be withdrawable if originally paid by customer. Promotional wallet credit is not withdrawable.
