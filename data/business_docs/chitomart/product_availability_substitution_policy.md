# ChitoMart Product Availability and Substitution Policy

## 1. Product Availability Overview

Product availability on ChitoMart depends on fulfillment hub stock, supplier updates, product expiry checks, quality checks, and demand.

Although ChitoMart tries to keep inventory updated in real time, stock may change quickly during peak hours, festivals, discounts, and supply shortages.

## 2. Stock Status Types

### 2.1 In Stock

Product is available at the selected fulfillment hub. Customer may order the item.

### 2.2 Low Stock

Product is available but quantity is limited. Order may be confirmed only after fulfillment check.

### 2.3 Out of Stock

Product is not available at the selected hub. Customer cannot order or may be offered substitution.

### 2.4 Temporarily Unavailable

Product is not available right now but may return soon.

### 2.5 Discontinued

Product is no longer sold by ChitoMart.

### 2.6 Area Restricted

Product is available only in selected areas due to delivery feasibility or cold-chain limits.

## 3. Why Product May Become Unavailable After Ordering

An item may become unavailable after order placement due to last unit sold before picker confirmation, damaged stock found during packing, expired product detected during quality check, supplier shortage, inventory sync delay, product reserved for another confirmed order, or cold-chain item unavailable in selected delivery zone.

## 4. Substitution Policy

If an ordered product is unavailable, ChitoMart may suggest a substitute.

Substitute product should be same product category, similar brand or quality, similar quantity or size, similar price range, not higher priced without customer approval, and suitable for customer's original need.

Customer confirmation is required before substitution unless the customer selected "allow substitutions" during checkout.

## 5. Price Difference in Substitution

If substitute is cheaper, customer pays lower price and difference is refunded or adjusted.

If substitute is more expensive, customer must approve additional cost. Without approval, original item should be removed or refunded.

If substitute has same price, order can continue after customer approval.

## 6. Fresh Item Substitution

For fresh items, substitution may involve similar vegetable, similar fruit, different size, different brand, or different packaging weight.

Support must not substitute allergy-sensitive or diet-sensitive items without confirmation.

Examples requiring confirmation:

1. Cow milk instead of buffalo milk
2. Sugar-free item instead of regular item
3. Baby formula brand change
4. Gluten-free product replacement
5. Eggless bakery substitution

## 7. Product Quality Check

Before dispatch, fulfillment team checks expiry date, packaging condition, seal condition, freshness, quantity, correct variant, correct brand, and visible damage.

If product fails quality check, it should not be delivered.

## 8. Expiry Date Policy

ChitoMart should not deliver expired products.

For products with short shelf life, ChitoMart should not deliver items that are too close to expiry unless clearly marked as clearance and accepted by customer.

If expired product is delivered, customer is eligible for refund or replacement after verification.

## 9. Product Description Mismatch

If product received does not match platform description, customer may be eligible for return or refund.

Mismatch examples include wrong size, wrong weight, wrong flavor, wrong variant, wrong quantity, wrong image causing confusion, wrong ingredient information, and wrong promotional bundle.

## 10. Product Image Disclaimer

Product images are for reference. Packaging may change depending on manufacturer updates. However, important differences such as size, quantity, flavor, brand, or variant must match the order.

## 11. Bundle and Offer Item Rules

If a customer orders a bundle or offer pack, all included items should be delivered.

If part of a bundle is missing, ChitoMart may deliver missing item, refund missing item value, offer replacement, or cancel bundle if incomplete.

Free gift items are subject to availability and may not be refundable.
