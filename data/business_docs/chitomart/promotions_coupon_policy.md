# ChitoMart Promotions and Coupon Policy

## 1. Promotion Overview

ChitoMart may offer coupons, wallet cashback, free delivery offers, festival discounts, category discounts, referral rewards, and bundle offers.

Promotions are subject to specific terms and may change without prior notice.

## 2. Coupon Eligibility

A coupon may depend on minimum order amount, customer account age, first-order status, product category, delivery area, payment method, order date, time of day, usage limit, and maximum discount cap.

## 3. Minimum Order Value

Some coupons require minimum order value.

Examples:

- Coupon `FIRST100`: minimum order NPR 1,000
- Coupon `GROCERY10`: minimum grocery order NPR 1,500
- Coupon `FREESHIP`: minimum order depends on area
- Coupon `WALLET50`: wallet payment required

Minimum order value is calculated after product discount but before delivery charge unless promotion says otherwise.

## 4. First Order Coupon

First order coupon is available only once per customer account, phone number, and device.

If customer cancels first order, coupon restoration depends on cancellation reason.

If cancellation is caused by ChitoMart issue, coupon may be restored.

If customer cancels voluntarily, coupon may not be restored.

## 5. Category Discount

Category discounts apply only to selected categories such as grocery, fresh vegetables, personal care, baby care, or beverage.

If cart contains mixed categories, discount applies only to eligible items.

## 6. Free Delivery Coupon

Free delivery coupon covers standard delivery charge only.

Free delivery coupon may not cover Priority Express delivery, heavy item surcharge, redelivery charge, remote area surcharge, or special handling fee.

## 7. Wallet Cashback

Wallet cashback is credited after successful delivery unless promotion says instant cashback.

Cashback may be delayed if order is not delivered, payment is pending, return/refund is under review, fraud check is triggered, or customer cancels order.

Promotional cashback is not withdrawable as cash.

## 8. Coupon Not Working

If customer reports coupon not working, check coupon code spelling, coupon expiry, minimum order amount, product category eligibility, customer eligibility, payment method, delivery area, whether coupon already used, maximum usage limit, and app version issue.

## 9. Coupon Abuse

ChitoMart may restrict coupons if customer creates multiple fake accounts, uses same device for many accounts, repeatedly cancels discounted orders, exploits referral program, or violates platform terms.

## 10. Refund and Coupon

If order is refunded fully due to ChitoMart issue, coupon may be restored or equivalent coupon may be issued.

If order is partially refunded, coupon restoration depends on remaining order value.

If customer cancels voluntarily, coupon may not be restored.
